Beehive Project

  • Product Overview
  • Details
  • Documentation

A few months ago, our team started thinking about the bee colony collapse problem. This is a significant issue, with dire implications, and yet there were very few comprehensive solutions on the table. Having failed quite epically at preventing the problem in the first place, we had little faith in regulators and official bodies acting fast.

This was an opportunity to help citizens solve the crisis quickly, and on their own terms. We began by recognising three important trends: maker culture, open source development andcitizen science.
