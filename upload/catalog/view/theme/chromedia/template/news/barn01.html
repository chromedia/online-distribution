Community Sundays at the OTC Farm

Building an Open Source Microfactory

On our 40 acre site near Denver, Colorado, we’re transforming an old barn built in the 60’s into our first manufacturing space. The renovation began in August, and we anticipate that we’ll be finished in the middle of Q1 2014.

The building has three bays and a second story room for a total of around 2,200 sf. When we began, it had a pretty intense lean to it from decades of minimal use and strong winds coming from the North.
