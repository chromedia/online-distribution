Yoonseo Speaks at the Seoul Digital Forum

Hey all, Yoonseo here. In May 2013 I presented at the Seoul Digital Forum (in Seoul, South Korea) which congregated people from around the world to speak publicly on different topics. The year's topic was ECOllaboration, to explore how an ecosystem of online and sustainable interaction would shape a collaborative future. The forum hosted presentations of founders and co-founders from the Honest Company, Open Source Ecology, Open Motors, and more.

Keynote speaker Tim Berners-Lee elaborated how the internet would grow layers of interaction upon itself. He emphasized the importance of royalty-free access to these systems- just as the internet grew wildly from its inception while unbound from excessive restrictions on public use.

Yooneseo Kang at the Seoul Digital Forum

I spoke on open source hardware, sharing my personal story and how open hardware can create vast creative and productive opportunities for individuals and communities. How open designs and information will revolutionize education and the economy, open hardware a critical digital-tangible key towards the 21st century collaborative society.
